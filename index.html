I am a research scientist at Qualcomm AI Research, living in Amsterdam. Previously, I was an applied scientist at Microsoft in London.

At Qualcomm, I have worked on 3D Computer Vision topics such as unsupervised positioning that is modality-agnostic and have applied it to WiFi positioning which has been featured at MWC 2021, IEEE Globecom 2021, and NeurIPS 2021. I am currently working on creating efficient on-device video-denoising algorithms.

My current research interests include self-supervised learning, positioning, and efficient video architecture design.
